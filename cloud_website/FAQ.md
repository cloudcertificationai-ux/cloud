## General Questions

### What is Anywheredoor?

Anywheredoor is an online learning platform that provides high-quality courses in web development, data science, design, and more. Our platform offers interactive video lessons, hands-on projects, and progress tracking to help you achieve your learning goals.

### Who can use Anywheredoor?

Anyone with an internet connection and a Google or Apple account can use Anywheredoor. Our courses are designed for learners of all levels, from beginners to advanced professionals.

### Is Anywheredoor free?

We offer both free and paid courses. Free courses provide full access to all lessons and materials. Paid courses require a one-time payment for lifetime access.

### What devices can I use?

Anywheredoor works on:
- Desktop computers (Windows, macOS, Linux)
- Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
- Mobile phones (iOS, Android)
- Any device with a modern web browser

### Do I need to download any software?

No! Anywheredoor is entirely web-based. You only need a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge) and an internet connection.

## Account & Authentication

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit the Anywheredoor website
2. Click "Sign In" in the top right corner
3. Choose "Sign in with Google" or "Sign in with Apple"
4. Grant permission to access your profile
5. Your account is created automatically!

### Can I use multiple accounts?

Each email address can have one account. If you sign in with different providers (Google and Apple) using the same email, they'll be linked to the same account.

### How do I change my email address?

Email addresses are tied to your social login provider (Google or Apple). To change your email:
1. Update your email with Google or Apple first
2. The change will sync automatically on your next login

### I forgot which account I used to sign up. What should I do?

Try both Google and Apple sign-in options. If you still can't access your account, contact support at support@yourdomain.com with your email address.

### Can I delete my account?

Yes. Contact support at support@yourdomain.com to request account deletion. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard security measures including:
- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure database storage
- Regular security audits

## Courses & Enrollment

### How many courses can I enroll in?

You can enroll in as many courses as you like! There's no limit to the number of courses you can take.

### Do courses expire?

No! Once you enroll in a course, you have lifetime access. You can learn at your own pace and revisit materials anytime.

### Can I preview a course before enrolling?

Yes! Course detail pages show:
- Course description and curriculum
- Instructor information
- Student reviews and ratings
- Sample lessons (for some courses)

### How do I enroll in a free course?

1. Navigate to the course detail page
2. Click "Enroll Now"
3. If you're not logged in, sign in first
4. Enrollment is completed automatically
5. Start learning immediately!

### How do I enroll in a paid course?

1. Navigate to the course detail page
2. Click "Enroll Now"
3. Sign in if you haven't already
4. You'll be redirected to the payment page
5. Enter your payment information
6. Complete the payment
7. Access the course immediately!

### Can I gift a course to someone?

Currently, we don't have a gift feature. However, you can purchase the course and share the login credentials with the recipient. Contact support for assistance.

### Can I transfer my enrollment to another person?

No, enrollments are non-transferable and tied to your account.

### What if I accidentally enrolled in the wrong course?

Contact support at support@yourdomain.com within 7 days of enrollment for assistance. Refund policies vary by course.

## Payments & Billing

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

### Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. We never store your credit card information on our servers.

### Can I get a refund?

Refund policies vary by course. Generally:
- **Within 7 days:** Full refund if you haven't completed more than 20% of the course
- **After 7 days:** No refunds, but contact support for special circumstances

### I was charged but didn't get access to the course. What should I do?

1. Check your email for a payment confirmation
2. Check your dashboard to see if the course appears
3. Try logging out and back in
4. If the issue persists, contact support with your payment confirmation

### Can I get an invoice or receipt?

Yes! Payment receipts are automatically sent to your email after each purchase. You can also download receipts from your profile page.

### Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes! We occasionally offer:
- Seasonal sales
- Bundle discounts
- Student discounts (with valid student ID)
- Corporate/bulk purchase discounts

Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on promotions.

### What currency are prices in?

Prices are displayed in INR (Indian Rupees) by default. We may support additional currencies in the future.

## Learning & Progress

### How do I access my enrolled courses?

1. Click "Dashboard" in the main navigation
2. All your enrolled courses are displayed
3. Click "Continue Learning" on any course to access it

### How is my progress tracked?

Progress is tracked automatically as you:
- Complete video lessons
- Finish readings
- Complete quizzes and assignments

Your progress is saved in real-time and synced across all devices.

### Can I download course videos?

Video downloads may be available for some courses. Look for the download icon in the course player. Downloaded videos are for personal use only.

### Can I take notes during lessons?

Some courses offer a built-in note-taking feature. You can also use external note-taking apps alongside the course player.

### What happens when I complete a course?

When you complete all lessons:
- The course is marked as "Completed" in your dashboard
- You receive a completion badge
- You may receive a certificate (if available for that course)
- You can still access all course materials

### Do I get a certificate?

Certificates are available for select courses upon completion. Check the course details page to see if a certificate is offered.

### Can I reset my progress?

Yes, contact support at support@yourdomain.com to reset your progress for a specific course. Note that this action cannot be undone.

### Can I speed up or slow down videos?

Yes! The video player supports playback speeds from 0.5x to 2x. Use the speed control in the player settings.

### Can I watch courses offline?

Currently, courses require an internet connection. We're working on offline viewing capabilities for the future.

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?

We support the latest versions of:
- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Apple Safari
- Microsoft Edge

### What internet speed do I need?

Minimum requirements:
- **Video streaming:** 2 Mbps
- **HD video:** 5 Mbps
- **Optimal experience:** 10+ Mbps

### Why won't videos play?

Common solutions:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Disable ad blockers
4. Clear browser cache
5. Update your browser to the latest version

See our [Troubleshooting Guide](./TROUBLESHOOTING_GUIDE.md) for more details.

### Why is my progress not saving?

Common solutions:
1. Ensure you're logged in
2. Check your internet connection
3. Wait a few seconds after completing a lesson
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Clear browser cache and try again

### Can I use Anywheredoor on mobile?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on mobile devices. Simply visit the website in your mobile browser.

### Is there a mobile app?

Currently, we offer a mobile-optimized website. A native mobile app may be available in the future.

### Why is the page loading slowly?

Common solutions:
1. Check your internet speed
2. Close unnecessary browser tabs
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Restart your device

### I'm getting an error message. What should I do?

1. Take a screenshot of the error
2. Note what you were doing when the error occurred
3. Try refreshing the page
4. If the error persists, contact support with the screenshot and details

## Admin Panel

### Who can access the admin panel?

Only users with the "ADMIN" role can access the admin panel. Contact your system administrator to request admin access.

### How do I access the admin panel?

Visit `https://admin.yourdomain.com` and sign in with your admin account.

### Can I manually enroll students?

Yes! Admins can manually enroll students in courses from the admin panel. This is useful for:
- Promotional access
- Refunds and re-enrollments
- Corporate training programs

### How do I view student progress?

1. Navigate to Students in the admin panel
2. Click on a student to view their details
3. View their enrollment history and progress for each course

### Can I export student data?

Yes! Use the "Export" button on the students page to download a CSV file with all student information.

### How often is data synchronized?

Data is synchronized in real-time between the main application and admin panel. Changes appear within seconds.

### Can I generate reports?

Yes! The admin panel offers various reports:
- Enrollment statistics
- Student growth metrics
- Course performance analytics
- Revenue reports (for paid courses)

### What are audit logs?

Audit logs track all administrative actions and important system events. They include:
- Who performed the action
- What was done
- When it occurred
- Additional context

Audit logs are useful for compliance, security audits, and troubleshooting.

## Privacy & Security

### What information do you collect?

We collect:
- Name and email (from your social login)
- Profile photo (from your social login)
- Learning progress and activity
- Payment information (processed by Stripe, not stored by us)

### How is my information used?

Your information is used to:
- Provide access to courses
- Track your learning progress
- Send important notifications
- Improve our platform
- Process payments

### Do you share my information?

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- Payment processing (Stripe)
- Email services (for notifications)
- Analytics (anonymized data only)

### Can I see what data you have about me?

Yes! Contact support at privacy@yourdomain.com to request a copy of your data.

### How do I update my privacy settings?

Privacy settings can be managed from your profile page. You can control:
- Email notifications
- Profile visibility
- Data sharing preferences

### Is my payment information stored?

No! Payment information is processed and stored securely by Stripe. We never store your credit card details on our servers.

### How long do you keep my data?

We retain your data as long as your account is active. After account deletion, we retain some data for legal and compliance purposes (typically 90 days).

### What security measures do you have?

We implement:
- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure database storage with encryption at rest
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Rate limiting and DDoS protection
- Automated backup and disaster recovery

### What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?

1. Change your password with your social login provider (Google or Apple)
2. Log out of all devices
3. Contact support immediately at security@yourdomain.com
4. Review your account activity
