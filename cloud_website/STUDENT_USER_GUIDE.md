- **Account:** Google or Apple account for authentication

### Supported Devices

## Creating an Account

### Step 1: Visit the Platform

Navigate to the anywheredoor website at `https://yourdomain.com`

### Step 2: Click Sign In

Click the "Sign In" button in the top right corner of the homepage.

### Step 3: Choose Your Login Method

You can sign in using:

**Google Account:**
1. Click "Sign in with Google"
2. Select your Google account
3. Grant permission to access your profile information
4. You'll be redirected back to the platform

**Apple Account:**
1. Click "Sign in with Apple"
2. Enter your Apple ID credentials
3. Choose whether to share your email
4. Grant permission to access your profile information
5. You'll be redirected back to the platform

### What Happens Next?

- Your account is automatically created
- Your profile is populated with information from your social account
- You're redirected to the student dashboard

## Enrolling in Courses

### Free Courses

1. Navigate to the course detail page
2. Click the "Enroll Now" button
3. If you're not logged in, you'll be redirected to sign in
4. After signing in, enrollment is completed automatically
5. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Paid Courses

1. Navigate to the course detail page
2. Click the "Enroll Now" button
3. If you're not logged in, you'll be redirected to sign in
4. After signing in, you'll be redirected to the payment page
5. Enter your payment information (Stripe checkout)
6. Complete the payment
7. Upon successful payment, you'll be enrolled automatically
8. You'll receive a confirmation email

### Enrollment Confirmation

After enrolling, you'll see:
- A success message
- The course appears in your dashboard
- Access to course materials is granted immediately

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### I can't log in

**Problem:** Login button doesn't work or redirects fail

**Solutions:**
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Ensure you're using the correct Google/Apple account
4. Check if your browser is blocking pop-ups
5. Disable browser extensions temporarily

#### I can't enroll in a course

**Problem:** Enrollment button doesn't work or shows an error

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you're logged in
2. Check if you're already enrolled (check your dashboard)
3. For paid courses, ensure your payment method is valid
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Contact support if the issue persists

#### Videos won't play

**Problem:** Video player shows an error or won't load

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps)
2. Try a different browser
3. Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers)
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try lowering the video quality
6. Ensure your browser supports HTML5 video

#### My progress isn't saving

**Problem:** Completed lessons don't show as complete

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you're logged in
2. Check your internet connection
3. Wait a few seconds after completing a lesson
4. Refresh the page to see updated progress
5. Try logging out and back in

#### I forgot which account I used to sign up

**Problem:** Can't remember if you used Google or Apple

**Solutions:**
1. Try both login methods
2. Check your email for welcome messages
3. Contact support with your email address

#### The page is loading slowly

**Problem:** Pages take a long time to load

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection speed
2. Close unnecessary browser tabs
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Restart your device

#### I can't access a course I enrolled in

**Problem:** Course doesn't appear in dashboard or shows "Not Enrolled"

**Solutions:**
1. Check if you completed the enrollment process
2. For paid courses, verify payment was successful
3. Check your email for enrollment confirmation
4. Try logging out and back in
5. Contact support with your course name and enrollment date

#### My profile information is incorrect

**Problem:** Name, email, or photo is wrong

**Solutions:**
1. Go to Profile > Edit Profile
2. Update your information manually
3. For email changes, you may need to create a new account
4. Profile photo syncs from your Google/Apple account

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Authentication

**Q: Can I use multiple accounts?**
A: Each email address can have one account. If you sign in with different providers (Google and Apple) using the same email, they'll be linked to the same account.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Email addresses are tied to your social login provider. To change your email, you'll need to update it with Google or Apple first.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support at support@yourdomain.com to request account deletion.

### Courses & Enrollment

**Q: Can I enroll in multiple courses?**
A: Yes! You can enroll in as many courses as you like.

**Q: Do courses expire?**
A: No, once enrolled, you have lifetime access to the course.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund policies vary by course. Check the course details or contact support.

**Q: Can I download course videos?**
A: Video downloads may be available for some courses. Check the course player for download options.

### Progress & Completion

**Q: How is progress calculated?**
A: Progress is based on the number of lessons completed divided by total lessons.

**Q: Do I get a certificate?**
A: Certificates are available for some courses upon completion. Check the course details.

**Q: Can I reset my progress?**
A: Contact support to reset your progress for a specific course.

### Technical

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Can I use the platform on mobile?**
A: Yes! The platform is fully responsive and works on mobile devices.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Currently, we offer a mobile-optimized website. A native app may be available in the future.
